# Move from hybrid (3 days in office) to full-time in office (5 days)

## Context

- Company is 320 people, 5 years old, currently hybrid: T/W/Th in office, M/F remote.
- Office is in a major US city; ~75% of employees live within commuting distance.
- Sentiment: engineering and design strongly prefer hybrid; sales and exec team prefer in-office.
- Productivity data is mixed — PRs per engineer-week unchanged since hybrid adoption; cross-functional collaboration anecdotally weaker.
- Attrition in last 12 months: 14%, with stated reasons including flexibility (~30% of exit interviews).
- A competitor recently announced full RTO and faced public attrition spike.

## What I'm trying to decide

Whether to mandate 5-day in-office attendance starting next quarter.

## Constraints

- Cannot grandfather indefinitely — needs a uniform policy.
- Need an answer within 6 weeks for HR communication purposes.
