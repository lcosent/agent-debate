# Kill Feature Y to redirect engineering capacity to a new strategic bet

## Context

- Feature Y has 12% of MAUs but contributes ~4% of revenue.
- Has remained flat for 18 months; product-led growth attempts have not moved the needle.
- Maintenance burden: ~3 engineers full-time on a 30-person product team.
- Customer-success team reports Feature Y is "expected" by enterprise customers — listed in 6 of the last 10 RFPs.
- The strategic bet (new product line) requires reassigning 4+ engineers within 30 days to hit a customer-validated launch date in 4 months.

## What I'm trying to decide

Whether to sunset Feature Y (with a 6-month support window) and redeploy engineering capacity, or keep it running and try to launch the new product more slowly.

## Constraints

- Killing Feature Y will generate customer complaints; some risk of churn.
- The new bet's customer validation is partly contingent on the launch timing.
